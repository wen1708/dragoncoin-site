About DragonCoin (DRC)

DragonCoin (DRC) is a community-driven digital value network deployed on the BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20). The project focuses on transparency, verifiable public information, and long-term ecosystem building.

1. What is DragonCoin?

DragonCoin is designed to be a recognizable on-chain identity and value token for future community ecosystems. By providing an official website, verified email, public contract, and social presence, the project makes it easy for explorers, exchanges and users to confirm that they are dealing with the official DragonCoin project.

3. Why BNB Smart Chain?

BNB Smart Chain offers low fees, broad wallet compatibility and good explorer support. This makes it easier for the DragonCoin team to maintain transparent project data and for users to verify information.

4. Transparency

DragonCoin maintains a minimal but complete set of public assets:

  • Official website with About & Whitepaper pages
  • Official X (Twitter) account for updates
  • Official email under the same domain
  • Separated EN / CN pages to avoid language confusion
  • Consistent logo files (favicon, token icon, banner)
