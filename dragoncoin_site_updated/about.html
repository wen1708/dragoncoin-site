About DragonCoin (DRC)

DragonCoin (DRC) is a community-driven digital value network deployed on the BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20). The goal is to provide a transparent, verifiable, and long-term friendly on-chain project for both users and reviewers.

1. What is DragonCoin?

DragonCoin is designed to be a recognizable on-chain identity and token brand for a future community ecosystem. By providing unified public information (website, email, contract, socials), the project makes it easy for explorers, exchanges, and users to confirm they are interacting with the official project.

2. Key Information

  • Token Name: DragonCoin
  • Symbol: DRC
  • Standard: BEP-20

3. Why BNB Smart Chain?

BNB Smart Chain offers low fees, wide wallet compatibility, and strong explorer support (BscScan). That makes it ideal for transparent, public-facing token projects.

DragonCoin will remain compatible with the BSC tooling ecosystem.

4. Transparency & Channels

To make it easy for both community and reviewers to verify the project, DragonCoin keeps the following public:

  • Official website (About + Whitepaper)
  • Official X (Twitter) account
  • Official email under the same domain
  • Separated EN / CN pages to avoid confusion
  • Consistent logo and image assets
