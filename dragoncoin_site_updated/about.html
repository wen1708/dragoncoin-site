3. Why BNB Smart Chain?

BNB Smart Chain offers low fees, wide wallet compatibility, and is well-supported by major block explorers such as BscScan. This makes it easier for the community to track token data, and for the team to provide transparent updates.

DragonCoin intends to stay compatible with the existing BSC tooling ecosystem.
